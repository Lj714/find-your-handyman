For customers

I need a handyman – post a job

This page is just for customers who need help with repairs, maintenance or small projects. Share a few details about your job and we’ll make it visible to local handymen.

Post a Job View available jobs

What you’ll be asked

The form will ask for a clear description of the work, where you’re based, and how and when you’d like to be contacted.

Details that help handymen

  • A short job title (e.g. “Replace leaking kitchen tap”).
  • Your area or postcode (no full address needed yet).
  • Any photos or extra notes you think are useful.
  • When you’d ideally like the work done.

The more specific you are, the easier it is for the right handyman to say yes.

What happens after you submit

  • Your job is stored securely in our system.
  • We review and approve suitable jobs.
  • Approved jobs appear on the public jobs list for handymen.
  • Handymen contact you directly using the details you provide.
