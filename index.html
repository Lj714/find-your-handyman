How it works

This platform is designed to work simply for both customers and handymen.

1. Tell us who you are

  • Customers: share the job you need help with.
  • Handymen: share the services you offer.

2. Add key details

  • Job location and rough timing.
  • Type of work, skills, or experience.
  • Anything important we should know.

3. Get connected

  • Customers are matched with suitable handymen where possible.
  • Handymen receive enquiries that fit their skills and area.

Benefits for both sides

For Customers

  • Stop scrolling through endless listings.
  • Explain your job once in a clear form.
  • Get help for repairs, small jobs and maintenance.

For Handymen

  • Receive enquiries that match your skills.
  • Less time wasted on unsuitable jobs.
  • Another way for local customers to find you.

For Everyone

  • A straightforward way to connect.
  • Less time wasted, more work completed.
  • Built with local communities in mind.
