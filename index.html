How it works

The aim is to keep things simple for both you and the handyman, so small jobs don’t turn into big headaches.

1. Tell me what you need

  • Small repairs and fixes
  • Maintenance jobs
  • Furniture assembly and similar tasks

2. I review your request

  • I look at the type of job and your location
  • If anything is unclear, I’ll follow up by email
  • I match the job to a suitable handyman where possible

3. You get a response

  • Hear back with next steps or a suggested handyman
  • Arrange time and details directly
  • Give feedback so the service can improve over time

Why this helps

For you

  • No long searches through dozens of listings.
  • Share your job once in a simple form.
  • Get a clearer starting point for small jobs.

For handymen

  • Requests come with basic job details upfront.
  • Less time spent on unsuitable enquiries.
  • Better match between skills and tasks.

For the service

  • Starts simple and can grow over time.
  • Room to add more features as needed.
  • Built around real requests and feedback.
