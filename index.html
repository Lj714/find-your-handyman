Concept project by Lucky Aigbefoh

A simple way to find a reliable handyman when you actually need one.

Instead of endless searching or asking around, this concept aims to give people a short, trusted list of local handymen they can contact quickly for small jobs and repairs.

Register your interest See how it could work

How it could work

The idea is to keep things simple for both the person needing help and the handyman providing it.

1. Choose your job type

  • Small repairs
  • Maintenance jobs
  • Furniture assembly

2. See local handymen

  • Short, clear profiles
  • Service area and type of work
  • Basic availability

3. Send a simple enquiry

  • Describe the job in a few sentences
  • Share preferred times
  • Receive a call or message back

Why this could help

For customers

  • No need to search through dozens of listings.
  • Clear, simple information about each handyman.
  • One place to start for small, everyday jobs.

For handymen

  • More focused, relevant enquiries.
  • Less time spent explaining what you do.
  • Better match between jobs and skills.

For the idea

  • Starts simple and can grow over time.
  • Room to add ratings, reviews, or pricing later.
  • Could expand to more areas and services.
