How it works

This platform works for both customers and handymen in a simple, structured way.

1. Choose your role

  • Need help? Submit a job request.
  • Handyman? Share the services you offer.

2. Provide key details

  • Customers describe the job and location.
  • Handymen specify skills, areas served, and availability.

3. Get connected

  • Customers receive suitable handyman recommendations.
  • Handymen receive job enquiries that match their skills.

Benefits for both sides

For Customers

  • No more searching through endless listings.
  • Share your job once and get matched.
  • Find reliable help for repairs and small jobs.

For Handymen

  • Receive relevant job requests.
  • Less back-and-forth with unsuitable enquiries.
  • Grow your client base simply by joining.

For Everyone

  • A simple, clear way to connect.
  • Less time wasted, more work completed.
  • Built to support local communities and small businesses.
